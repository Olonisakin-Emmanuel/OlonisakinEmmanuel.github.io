I enjoy designing end-to-end AI systems, from data preprocessing and model development to building user-facing applications

Beyond engineering, I am passionate about knowledge sharing—teaching and mentoring in Python for Data Analysis, SQL, Power BI, Tableau and Excel with a focus on practical, real-world applications.
